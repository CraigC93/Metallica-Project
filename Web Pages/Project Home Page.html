About Metallica

Metallica is an American heavy metal band.
The band was formed in 1981 in Los Angeles by vocalist/guitarist James Hetfield and drummer Lars Ulrich, and has been based in San Francisco for most of its career.

This Month in Metallica History

This month in 2019, Metallica played live in Slane Castle, Slane, Co. Meath,Ireland. (June 8th 2019).
This was the 12th time Metallica have performed in Ireland, since they first played here in 1986.
This was the first time that Metallica had performed at Slane and their first concert in Ireland in nearly ten years.
They last played in Marlay Park in Dublin in 2009.
